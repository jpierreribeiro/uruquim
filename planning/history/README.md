# planning/history — superseded working documents

These are the working artifacts of already-frozen phases: prototypes, evidence
records, audits and benchmark shootouts. They are preserved for anyone who
needs to re-read *how* a decision was reached — but they are **not** the durable
record of a phase. **The durable record of each phase is its
`planning/phase-N-freeze.md`** (top level), and the current governance,
backlogs and ADRs also live at the top level.

Moved here by the process remodel (`planning/process-remodel.md`, 2026-07-23) so
the top-level `planning/` view is governance + freezes + the plans in flight,
not a mix of live rules and captured scaffolding. Nothing here is gate-
referenced; moving or reading it changes no build behaviour.
